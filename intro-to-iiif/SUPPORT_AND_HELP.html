Getting Ongoing Support and Technical Help

Getting Ongoing Support and Technical Help

The IIIF Awesome List is a great resource to find software, applications, and other fun stuff IIIF.

There are several ways to get involved with the IIIF community and get IIIF help. The IIIF Community page is a great place to get started.

  • IIIF-discuss is a Google Group that is a good place to start with asking questions and discussing IIIF.
  • Join the IIIF Slack team
  • http://iiif.io/community/call participate in a bi-weekly teleconference

Community Groups

IIIF has community and technical groups that you can get involved with.

  • A/V Technical Specification Group
  • Discovery Technical Specification Group
  • Manuscripts Community Group
  • Museums Community Group
  • Newspapers Community Group
  • Software Developers Community Group
